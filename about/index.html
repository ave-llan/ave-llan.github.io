I'm John Leszczynski, a composer and programmer with an interest in birds and other things.

My music has been performed throughout the country by groups such as the US Navy Band, US Army Field Band, the Barkada Quartet, Zzyzx Quartet, and the Akropolis Quintet.

I work at Google as a Software Engineer in Seattle.
